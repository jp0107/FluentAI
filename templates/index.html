OUR MISSION

Providing students with frequent and varied opportunities to practice their language skills and to receive valuable, informative feedback is an essential part of second-language acquisition, yet it is often difficult to integrate into traditional course curriculum. FluentAI aims to solve this common problem in language learning by giving students opportunities to converse in a foreign language while also providing them with timely feedback. By leveraging the ChatGPT API, we allow professors to input pre-written prompts with grading criteria. Students can then converse with the chatbot through short passages of written text, receiving corrections and assessments on their grammar, fluency, and lexical diversity.
